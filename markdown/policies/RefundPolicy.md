## 1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are offered **only** if:

- The item arrives **damaged**,
- The item is **defective**, or
- The order is **not received within 30 business days**

In these cases, a full refund or replacement will be processed.

---

## 2. Non-Refundable Cases

We cannot offer refunds or returns due to **sizing issues**.  
Please carefully review the sizing charts before ordering.

We encourage customers to contact support for fit guidance if unsure.

---

## 3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund:

1. Provide your order number
2. Include photos/videos of damaged or defective items (if applicable)
3. Our team will review and provide a resolution promptly

---

## 4. Customer Responsibilities

- Ensure accurate shipping information at checkout
- Review product details and sizes carefully before purchasing
